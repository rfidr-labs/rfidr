  • What is RFIDR?

    As a token built on the Polygon Network and Binance Smart Chain (BEP-20), RFIDR is pegged based on a 1:1000 ratio of all types of IDR Stable Coins born from Blockchain Technology.

    In this way, your IDR can be easily integrated with blockchain services, of course not directly but through an intermediary between LOCAL IDR-STABLE COIN IDR which uses Blockchain Technology - RFIDR Connection.

  • Where can I take advantage of RFIDR now ?

    Currently RFIDR can be used to facilitate IDR Crypto Stable Coin Token Owners obtained on exchanges such as IDRX via INDODAX & NUSA, IDRT via PINTU and IDDR via Exchange Channels where IDDR Listing.

    Additionally, users can also use RFIDR to access several other decentralized financial platform service features in our Blockchain Alliance Project.

    Going forward, the RFIDR ecosystem will continue to develop and expand its use across the Global DEFI network.

  • How to get RFIDR ?

    RFIDR can only be owned in 4 ways, namely:

    01. Get RFIDR via the RFIDR official at wwww.rfidr.co by sending the IDR crypto base you have to us. The RFIDR that will be received is equivalent to the value of the Crypto IDR that you send in a ratio of 1:1000, (1 RFIDR = 1000 IDR Stable Coin)

    02. Get RFIDR via DexTrade at wwww.plaxswap.io (Polygon) or wwww.cryptopreneur.finance (BEP20).

    03. Get RFID via Does Reward Pool, Token Gift, or Reward Lock Holder from Hodlers participate in the RFIDR LOCK HODLER Revenue Share Program.

    03. Get RFID via Peer to Peer Transaction With Everyone.

  • How does RFIDR flow and work if it goes through Official RFIDR?

    The method is very simple, easy & very short.

    01. Please visit our official website and make sure you have an IDR Stable Coin Balance (IDRX, IDRT or IDDR) which you got in 4 ways as explained in the previous point.

    02. On the Getting Started page, please send the Stable Coin IDR balance that you have from the 3 available options to us.

    03. Within a maximum period of 1x24 hours after we receive the Blockhash Trx from you, we will Minting an RFIDR which is equivalent to the IDR Stable Coin you sent.

    The RFIDR that will be received is equivalent to the value of the Crypto IDR that you send in a ratio of 1:1000
    (1 RFIDR = 1000 Crypto Stable Coin IDR).

    04. Congratulation, You Receive RFIDR in your wallet after Blockchain Minting Confirmation is Done

    05. - To maintain supply stability, we will periodically and continuously burn RFIDR sourced from the DexTrade Buyback Guard Market which is technically tasked with reducing the total circulating supply in order to maintain the stability of the RFIDR value.
